# UI inspiration: Google Ads (business.google.com)

Learnings from [Google Ads](https://business.google.com/us/google-ads/) and how to apply them to AdvertisingSystems.

---

## What Google Ads does well

### 1. **Hero: one clear AI value prop**
- **Headline:** “Let AI find the customers you’ve been missing.”
- **Subtext:** Short line on what the product does (AI, signals, “next best customer”).
- **CTAs:** Primary (“Start now”) + secondary (“Chat with Google Ads”).
- **Takeaway:** One sentence that says “AI + outcome,” then one line of proof, then two clear actions.

### 2. **Offer / incentive above the fold**
- “New to Google Ads? Choose an offer to earn up to $X in ad credit.”
- **Takeaway:** Lead with a concrete benefit (credit, trial, guarantee) near the hero.

### 3. **Product as a set of “ways to be seen”**
- Section: “Google Ads gives you many ways to be seen” with **Performance Max, Search, Display, Shopping, Video, App**.
- Each has: icon, short title, one-line benefit, “Explore X” CTA.
- **Takeaway:** Homepage clearly shows “product pillars” with icon + one line + one link each.

### 4. **Offer selector (conversion-focused)**
- “Choose a sign-up offer to jumpstart your first campaign.”
- OFFER A / B / C with credit amount and spend requirement; “Claim Now.”
- **Takeaway:** If you have tiers or offers, make them selectable in one place with one main CTA.

### 5. **Goals section**
- “Achieve all your goals in one place” with cards: Maximize leads, Increase sales, Foot traffic, Brand awareness, App marketing.
- **Takeaway:** Frame features by **outcome** (leads, sales, traffic) not only by feature name.

### 6. **Three pillars of value**
- “The power of Google, for your business”: Reach / Track & optimize ROI / Full control of budget.
- **Takeaway:** One section with 3 clear value pillars (reach, measurement, control).

### 7. **Social proof with numbers and video**
- “What success looks like” — customer logos, big numbers (23x ROI, 400% ROI, 150% YoY, 675% ROAS), short quote, “Watch their story.”
- **Takeaway:** Combine logo + one metric + one quote + optional video link.

### 8. **Help / support block**
- “Let us help” — free personalized support, **Call** and **Schedule a meeting** and **Chat**.
- **Takeaway:** One section that surfaces “talk to us” (call, meeting, chat) clearly.

### 9. **FAQ: expand/collapse**
- “Frequently asked questions” with expand/collapse and “Expand all / Collapse all.”
- **Takeaway:** FAQ with accordion and a single “expand all” control.

### 10. **Footer structure**
- Columns: Products, Learning & support, Partners, More solutions + locale/language.
- **Takeaway:** Group links by “what you get,” “help,” “partners,” “other.”

## Recommended UI updates for AdvertisingSystems

### High impact (do first)

| Area | Current | Google Ads–style change |
|------|--------|--------------------------|
| **Hero incentive** | “No credit card · 14-day trial · Cancel anytime” | Add one line above or beside CTAs: e.g. “Start free — no credit card. 14-day trial.” or a small “Earn $X in ad credit” style offer if you have one. |
| **Product pillars on homepage** | Features section with 3 blocks (Campaign Intelligence, AI Optimization, Analytics, etc.) | Add a **compact “Ways we help”** section: 4–6 cards (Campaign Intelligence, AI Optimization, Analytics, Automation, Budget, OTAs), each with icon + one line + “Explore” link, similar to “Performance Max, Search, Display…” |
| **Goals / outcomes** | Pain points + features | Add one section: **“Achieve your goals in one place”** with 4–5 outcome cards: “Maximize ROAS,” “Lower CPA,” “Unify Google, Meta & OTAs,” “Save time,” “Scale without overages.” |
| **Support block** | Contact link in nav/footer | Add a **“Let us help”** strip before footer: “Get started with a free demo. Book a call · Chat · Email” with clear Book demo + Contact links. |
| **FAQ** | FAQ section | Add **“Expand all / Collapse all”** to the FAQ component so users can open/close every item at once. |

### Medium impact

| Area | Recommendation |
|------|----------------|
| **Hero subtext** | Keep “The AI advertising engine that unifies…” but consider one even shorter line directly under the headline (e.g. “One dashboard. Every channel.”). |
| **Testimonials** | Where you have case-style content, add one **big number per story** (e.g. “5.2x ROAS,” “38% lower CPA”) and a “Read full story” or “Watch” if you add video. |
| **Pricing** | If you have multiple plans, consider a single “Choose your plan” section with clear “Start free” / “Claim offer” style CTAs per plan. |
| **Footer** | Group links into columns: **Product** (Features, Pricing, OTA, Integrations), **Support** (FAQ, Contact, Book demo), **Company** (About, Blog, Careers), **Legal** (Privacy, Terms). |

### Copy / messaging (no layout change)

- Use a **single headline that ties “AI” to “outcome”** (you already have “Your ads should Make money…” — could add a variant like “Let AI find the ad performance you’ve been missing.” for A/B or alternate sections).
- Use **“Start now”** or **“Start free”** as primary CTA where it fits (you already have “Start Free Trial”).
- Use **“Schedule a meeting”** or **“Book a call”** next to “Book a Demo” where it fits.
